# Ride the Staff

Hop on the staff and fly around like a broomstick. Bind the ride key in **Options → Controls →
Command Block Staff Addon**, then press it while holding the staff to mount; press again to dismount.

## Flying

- Look where you want to go and move — the staff carries you, leaning into climbs and dives.
- A **firework trail** streams from the staff tip as you fly (fully tunable — see below).
- Other players can see you riding in multiplayer.

## Crashing

Slam into the ground at speed and you'll be thrown off — the staff pops out of your hand and you dismount.

## Tuning the trail

The particle trail's spawn offset, spread, count and "shoot-out" direction are adjustable in the settings —
dial it in to taste.

!!! tip
    Riding uses **GeckoLib** for the animation, so make sure GeckoLib is installed (it's a required
    dependency).
