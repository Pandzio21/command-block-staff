# Ride the Staff

Bind a ride key under **Options → Controls**, then press it while holding the staff to mount; press again to
dismount. Look where you want to go and move — the staff carries you, leaning into climbs and dives, with a
firework trail off the tip (offset, spread, count and shoot-out direction are all tunable).

Hit the ground at speed and you're thrown off — the staff pops out of your hand. Other players see you
riding. Needs GeckoLib.
