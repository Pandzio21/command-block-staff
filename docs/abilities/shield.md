# Shield (Ability 5)

Raise a translucent force-field dome around yourself. While it's up you take **no damage**, and harmful
projectiles **bounce off** it.

## Controls

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Right-click | Toggle the shield on / off |
| {{ "`" }} + scroll | Shield size (radius 1.5 → 4) |

## Behaviour

- **Damage immunity** — all incoming damage is cancelled while shielded, and explosions are fully ignored
  (including knockback).
- **Barrier** — living mobs and players inside get pushed out; dropped items and XP pass through.
- **Deflection** — incoming harmful projectiles bounce off with a spark instead of being eaten.
- **Shape morph** — small shields look like a tall egg; large ones round out into a dome. Surface wobbles
  like a real force field (amount is configurable).
- **Durability (optional)** — turn on *Shield durability* and the shield absorbs a set amount of damage,
  then **shatters** with a glass break.
- Clears automatically on death so it doesn't linger through respawn.

Other players can see your dome (and its colour/wobble) in multiplayer.
