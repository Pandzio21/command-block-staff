# Beam (Ability 1)

A tractor beam that grabs an entity and holds it at the tip of your staff. Whip the camera around and the
entity follows; let go to fling it.

## Modes

Cycle with ++left-alt++:

- **Hold** — *hold* right-click to grab. The entity tracks your crosshair while you hold. Release to drop
  it — and if you were swinging, it gets **thrown** in that direction. Throw it into the ground or a wall
  and it takes **impact damage**.
- **Bound** — right-click **sticks** the entity to the staff tip so it follows you hands-free. Right-click
  again to release. Bound mode won't bury mobs inside walls.

## Controls

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Right-click (Hold) | Grab while held; release to drop / throw |
| Right-click (Bound) | Toggle grab / release |
| {{ "`" }} + scroll | Beam distance |

## Notes

- The beam is drawn as a clean glowing line from your staff to the entity, with a rising whoosh as it
  moves faster.
- A small command billboard floats over the grabbed entity (you can turn billboard text off in settings).
- Works on players and mobs.
